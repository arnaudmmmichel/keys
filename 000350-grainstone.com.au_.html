Over 1.3 billion tonnes of food waste biomass is produced each year, whilst simultaneously over 700 million people are undernourished. It’s madness. Consumers want healthier, more sustainable, interesting foods. Consumers want biodegradable bioplastics and sustainable textiles. We bioprospect and mine traditional food waste streams using our advanced foodtech and biotech to help create the most valuable products possible, prioritising food creation, and displacing unsustainable alternatives. We are starting with grain byproducts, particularly brewers spent grain, where we are deploying the missing technology links in value chains. Over 45 million tonnes of brewers spent grain is produced globally each year that is under-utilised, usually sent to landfill or to farmers as a stock feed. Brewing beer only removes the starchy sugars from the grain, yet the food-grade byproduct is all the fantastic protein, fibre and micronutrients that goes to waste.
